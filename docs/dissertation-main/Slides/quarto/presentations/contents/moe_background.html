Switching Control with Deep-net Mixture of Experts

Background

  • Mixture of Experts

Mixture of Experts (MOE): Old Faithful

  • Objective: learn two expert Gaussian models that represent dataset \(\mathbb{D} = \{(x_1, y_1), \dots, (x_N, y_N) \}\)
  • The experts are chosen as \[\begin{align*} F(x;\theta) &= \{\mathcal{N}_1(\mu_1, \sigma_1), \mathcal{N}_2(\mu_2, \sigma_2)\} \\ \theta &= \{(\mu_1, \sigma_1), (\mu_2, \sigma_2) \} \end{align*}\]
    • Prediction is a weighted-average of experts

  • The gating network is a neural net \(\mathbf{P}(x | \psi) = [P_1(x | \psi), P_2(x | \psi) ]\)
    • Divides the input space into partitions
  • Expectation Maximization: maximizes log likelihood \[ \ln \{P(\mathbb{D} | \theta, \psi) \} = \sum_{j=1}^{N} \ln \sum_{i=1}^{N_F} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \sigma_i^2}} \exp \left( -\frac{1}{2}\frac{(y_j - \mu_i)^2}{\sigma_i^2} \right) P_i(x_j, \psi) \]
